Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

StockDisplay ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

User-Provided Information

When you use StockDisplay, you may provide:

  • Stock symbols and names you choose to track
  • Custom API configurations you set up
  • App settings and preferences

All such information is stored locally on your device using SwiftData and is not transmitted to our servers.

Automatically Collected Information

We do not collect any automatically gathered information such as usage analytics, device identifiers, or crash reports.

Third-Party APIs

StockDisplay fetches stock price data from third-party APIs that you configure within the app (such as Yahoo Finance or custom API endpoints). Please note:

  • We do not control and are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services
  • Your interactions with third-party APIs are governed by their respective privacy policies
  • Stock symbols and configuration data you provide may be sent to the APIs you configure

Data Storage

All data you create with StockDisplay (stock configurations, data sources, app settings) is stored locally on your device. This data is not synced to any cloud services or external servers managed by us.

Data Sharing

We do not share any personal information with third parties. The stock data you view is fetched directly from the APIs you configure, and we do not intercept, store, or transmit this data.

Children's Privacy

StockDisplay is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
